Volleyball

The Volleyball club welcomes players of all levels!

Recreational activity:

Never played before? No problem!

Volleyball is one of the top recreational activities available on campus. Whether you are looking to play for fun ,fitness ,or to kickstart your volleyball career, our recreational activities are just for you!

Performance teams :

For you big hitters ,blockers and hand setters we have a women’s team and a men’s team. We represent IITH in INTER IIT SPORTSMEET and a few other tournaments. We provide position specific training and an amazing challenging environment.

Join our volleyball team and contribute to our success.
