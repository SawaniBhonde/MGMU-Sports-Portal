Aquatics

Welcome to the thrilling world of Para Aquatics at MGM University! Our Para Aquatics program is designed to foster inclusivity and empower athletes with disabilities to achieve their aquatic dreams. Para Aquatics encompasses a diverse range of swimming events and water-based activities tailored for individuals with physical, visual, and intellectual impairments.
